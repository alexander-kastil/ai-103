The StudioLink 90 is Fabrikam Audio's premium wireless over-ear headphone, blending reference-grade tuning with adaptive noise cancellation. 40mm dynamic drivers deliver balanced, detailed sound, while plush memory-foam earcups keep long listening sessions comfortable.

- **Adaptive ANC**: automatically adjusts noise cancellation to your environment

- **Hi-Res wireless**: LDAC and aptX Adaptive codecs for near-lossless streaming
- **Multipoint pairing**: connect to two devices (e.g. laptop and phone) simultaneously
- **40-hour battery**: 5-minute quick charge gives 5 hours of playback
