## ResonX 200 Bookshelf Speakers (Pair)

**SKU:** FA-RX200-BLK
**Price:** $329.99
**Category:** Passive Bookshelf Speakers
**Finish:** Matte Black / Walnut
**Impedance:** 6 ohms

### Description
The ResonX 200 is Fabrikam Audio's flagship bookshelf speaker, engineered for audiophiles who want studio-grade clarity in a compact cabinet. A 5.25-inch woven-fiber woofer pairs with a 1-inch silk-dome tweeter to deliver detailed highs and a surprisingly full low end. Ideal for small to medium rooms and stereo two-channel listening.

### Key Features
- **Woven-fiber woofer**: stiff yet light cone for fast, accurate bass response
- **Silk-dome tweeter**: smooth, fatigue-free treble even at high volume
- **Rear bass port**: extends low-frequency output down to 48 Hz
- **Bi-wire binding posts**: gold-plated, accept banana plugs, spades, or bare wire
- **MDF cabinet**: internally braced to eliminate resonance and coloration

### Specifications
| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| Frequency response | 48 Hz – 22 kHz |

### What's Included
- 2 ResonX 200 speakers
- 2 magnetic grille covers
- Rubber isolation feet

### FAQ
**Do I need an amplifier or receiver?** Yes. The ResonX 200 is a passive speaker and requires an external amplifier or AV receiver rated between 25 and 120 watts per channel.

**Can I wall-mount these?** They include threaded inserts on the rear for compatible wall brackets (sold separately, model FA-WB1).

## AeroSound Mini Wireless Speaker

**SKU:** FA-AM-CHR
**Price:** $129.99
**Category:** Portable Wireless Speakers
**Finish:** Charcoal / Sky Blue / Sand
**Battery:** 16 hours

### Description
The AeroSound Mini is a rugged, take-anywhere Bluetooth speaker built for the outdoors and the bookshelf alike. Despite its palm-sized footprint, a dual passive-radiator design produces room-filling sound with punchy bass. IP67 rating means it survives rain, dust, and a dunk in the pool.

### Key Features
- **Bluetooth 5.3**: stable wireless range up to 30 m
- **IP67 waterproof and dustproof**: fully submersible to 1 m for 30 minutes
- **Stereo pairing**: link two AeroSound Mini units for true left/right stereo
- **USB-C fast charge**: full charge in 2.5 hours
- **Built-in mic**: hands-free speakerphone with echo cancellation

### Specifications
| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|

| Battery life | 16 hours at 50% volume |
| Bluetooth | 5.3, AAC + SBC codecs |
| Water/dust rating | IP67 |

### FAQ
**Can I connect it to my TV?** The AeroSound Mini is Bluetooth-only and best suited for music. For TV audio, see the Fabrikam soundbar range.

**How many can I pair together?** Up to two units in stereo mode, or up to eight units in party (mono broadcast) mode.
