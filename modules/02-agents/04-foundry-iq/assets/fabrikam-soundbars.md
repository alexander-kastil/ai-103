## CineBar 500 Soundbar with Wireless Subwoofer

**SKU:** FA-CB500-BLK
**Price:** $449.99
**Category:** 3.1 Channel Soundbars
**Finish:** Black
**HDMI:** eARC

### Description
The CineBar 500 transforms any flat-panel TV into a home cinema. A dedicated center channel locks dialogue in place while left and right channels widen the soundstage. The included wireless subwoofer adds cinematic low-end impact without running cables across the room.

### Key Features
- **3.1 channel configuration**: left, right, dedicated center, plus wireless subwoofer
- **HDMI eARC**: single-cable connection passes lossless audio from the TV
- **Dolby Audio decoding**: optimized surround processing for movies and streaming
- **Night mode**: compresses dynamic range so loud scenes don't wake the house
- **Bluetooth 5.2**: stream music directly from a phone or tablet

### FAQ
**Does it support Dolby Atmos?** The CineBar 500 supports Dolby Audio but not Atmos height channels. For Atmos, see the CineBar 900 Pro below.

**Will the subwoofer pair automatically?** Yes. The subwoofer ships pre-paired and connects wirelessly when both units power on. A manual re-pair button is on the subwoofer's rear panel.

**Which TV connection should I use?** HDMI eARC offers the best quality and supports TV remote volume control. Use optical only if your TV lacks an eARC/ARC HDMI port.

## CineBar 900 Pro Dolby Atmos Soundbar System

**SKU:** FA-CB900-BLK
**Price:** $899.99
**Category:** 5.1.2 Channel Soundbars
**Finish:** Black
**HDMI:** 4K eARC passthrough

### Description
The CineBar 900 Pro is Fabrikam Audio's reference home-theater system. Up-firing height drivers bounce sound off the ceiling for true Dolby Atmos object-based audio, while two wireless rear surround speakers complete an immersive 5.1.2 setup. Built for dedicated home theaters and large living rooms.

### Key Features
- **5.1.2 channel system**: soundbar, wireless subwoofer, and two wireless rear surrounds
- **Up-firing Atmos drivers**: two height channels for overhead effects
- **4K/120Hz HDMI passthrough**: gaming-ready, supports VRR and ALLM
- **Auto room calibration**: built-in mic measures the room and tunes output
- **Voice clarity engine**: boosts dialogue intelligibility independently of effects

### Specifications

### FAQ
**Do the rear speakers need power outlets?** Yes. The rear surrounds are wireless for audio but each requires a nearby power outlet.

**Can I add this to an existing AV receiver?** The CineBar 900 Pro is a self-contained system and connects directly to your TV via HDMI eARC. It does not require a separate receiver.
