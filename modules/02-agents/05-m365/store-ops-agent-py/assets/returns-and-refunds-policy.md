# Returns &amp; Refunds Policy

**Contoso Retail — Store Operations**
Effective: January 2026
Owner: Customer Experience Team
Document version: 5.0

This policy tells store associates and managers how to process customer returns, exchanges, and refunds at any Contoso Retail location.

---

## Standard return window

- **Most merchandise:** 30 days from the purchase date with a valid receipt.
- **Contoso loyalty members:** 60 days from the purchase date (membership verified at the register).
- **Holiday purchases:** items bought between November 1 and December 24 may be returned through January 15 of the following year.

A purchase date is the date printed on the receipt or recorded against the loyalty account, not the date the item was first used.

## Proof of purchase

| Proof provided | What you can do |
|---|---|
| Original receipt | Full refund to the original payment method |
| Loyalty account lookup (no paper receipt) | Full refund to the original payment method |
| Gift receipt | Exchange or store credit at the current selling price |
| No proof of purchase | Store credit at the **lowest price** in the last 30 days; requires a manager approval and a government photo ID |

## Refund method rules

- **Card purchases** are refunded to the **same card**. If the card is unavailable, issue store credit — never refund a card purchase as cash.
- **Cash purchases** under $100 are refunded as cash. Cash refunds of $100 or more are issued as a check mailed from head office within 7 business days.
- **Gift card purchases** are refunded to a new gift card.
- **Split-tender purchases** (part card, part cash) are refunded proportionally to each method.

## Items that cannot be returned

The following are **final sale** and may not be returned or exchanged:

- Opened software, digital download cards, and activated gift cards.
- Personalized or custom-ordered items.
- Clearance items marked "Final Sale" on the receipt.
- Perishable goods and opened consumables.
- Undergarments and earrings, for hygiene reasons.

Items returned within the window but **opened or used** may be accepted at the manager's discretion with a restocking fee (see below).

## Restocking fees

- A **15% restocking fee** applies to opened electronics returned in working condition.
- No restocking fee for unopened items, defective items, or items returned because of a Contoso error.
- Restocking fees are waived for loyalty members on their first return each calendar year.

## Defective and recalled items

- **Defective items** are always eligible for a full refund or free replacement regardless of the return window, with proof of purchase.
- **Recalled items** are accepted for a full refund at any time, with or without a receipt. Follow the recall notice posted in the back office and quarantine returned units in the recall bin — do not restock them.

## Manager approval thresholds

| Action | Who can approve |
|---|---|
| Return within policy, with receipt | Any associate |
| Return without proof of purchase | Shift manager |
| Refund over $250 | Shift manager |
| Refund over $1,000 | Store manager |
| Override of a final-sale restriction | Store manager (record reason code) |

## Processing a return at the register

1. Choose **Transactions > Return** from the register menu.
2. Scan the receipt barcode, or look up the purchase by loyalty account or card.
3. Scan or select the items being returned and confirm their condition.
4. Apply any restocking fee or manager override as required, recording the reason code.
5. Confirm the refund method shown matches the rules above before finalizing.
6. Hand the customer the printed return receipt and place returned stock in the correct bin: **restock**, **damage-out**, or **recall**.
