# Europe Travel Destinations Guide

## Southern Europe (Best: June–October)

### Greece
- **Athens**: Acropolis, Parthenon, Plaka neighbourhood. Best October for mild weather and fewer crowds.
- **Santorini**: Caldera views, volcanic beaches (Perissa, Kamari). Best late September — water warm, crowds thinning.
- **Crete**: Samaria Gorge hike, Heraklion Palace of Knossos, southern beaches. Good until end of October.
- **Climate**: Mediterranean. July–August are hottest (32–38°C) and busiest. October sits at 22–26°C.

### Portugal
- **Lisbon**: Alfama district, tram 28, Belém Tower, Jerónimos Monastery. Excellent year-round.
- **Algarve**: Praia da Marinha sea caves, Benagil Cave boat tours. Waters warm through October (21°C).
- **Porto**: Ribeira district, Douro Valley wine tours, Livraria Lello bookshop.
- **Climate**: Atlantic. October is mild (18–22°C), some rain, very affordable.

### Spain
- **Barcelona**: Sagrada Família, Park Güell, Gothic Quarter, La Boqueria. Busy in summer; October is ideal.
- **Seville**: Alcázar palace, flamenco, tapas culture. October is excellent — summer heat dissipates.
- **Mallorca**: Cala d'Or, Serra de Tramuntana mountain villages. Good through October.
- **Climate**: Varies. Northern Spain cooler; Andalusia hot summers, mild October.

### Italy
- **Rome**: Colosseum, Vatican, Trevi Fountain, Trastevere. October is perfect — 20°C, fewer crowds.
- **Amalfi Coast**: Positano, Ravello, boat trips to Capri. Swimming still possible in October.
- **Venice**: Doge's Palace, Grand Canal, vaporetto routes. October pleasant before acqua alta season.
- **Tuscany**: Florence, Siena, Chianti wine region, San Gimignano towers. Harvest season in October.
- **Climate**: Mediterranean. October temperatures 18–22°C across the south.

## Central Europe (Best: May–September)

### Austria
- **Vienna**: Kunsthistorisches Museum, Schönbrunn Palace, Naschmarkt, coffee house culture.
- **Salzburg**: Mozart's birthplace, Hohensalzburg Fortress, Hellbrunn Palace trick fountains.
- **Climate**: Continental. October cooler (10–15°C), autumnal colours in mountains.

### Czech Republic
- **Prague**: Old Town Square and Astronomical Clock, Charles Bridge, Josefov. Busy summer; October quieter and golden-lit.
- **Český Krumlov**: UNESCO-listed castle town, one-hour drive from Prague.
- **Climate**: Continental. October mild (8–14°C).

### Hungary
- **Budapest**: Buda Castle, Chain Bridge, Széchenyi Baths, ruin bars. October is lovely — thermal baths feel more appealing.
- **Climate**: Continental. October 10–16°C, occasional rain.

## Western Europe (Good year-round)

### France
- **Paris**: Eiffel Tower, Louvre, Marais, Montmartre, Versailles day trip.
- **French Riviera**: Nice, Cannes, Antibes, Èze. Sea swimmable until October (21°C).
- **Provence**: Lavender (blooms July), Gorges du Verdon, Avignon Palais des Papes.
- **Climate**: Paris rainy in autumn; South of France warm through October.

### Netherlands
- **Amsterdam**: Rijksmuseum, Anne Frank House, canal cycling, Jordaan district. October sees autumn foliage along canals.
- **Climate**: Temperate maritime. October 10–15°C, prepare for rain.

## Practical Travel Notes

### Visa Requirements
- **Schengen Zone**: EU/EEA citizens no visa. Non-EU visitors from many countries get 90 days within 180-day period without visa.
- **ETIAS**: From mid-2025, non-EU visitors (including US, UK, Australia) need ETIAS travel authorisation (€7, valid 3 years).

### Currency
- **Euro (€)**: Used in France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Greece, Austria, Netherlands, and most of Western/Southern Europe.
- **Non-Euro**: UK (GBP), Czech Republic (CZK), Hungary (HUF), Switzerland (CHF), Croatia (EUR since 2023).

### Getting Around
- **Rail**: Eurail pass covers 33 countries. High-speed trains (TGV, Eurostar, Frecciarossa, AVE) connect major cities.
- **Budget airlines**: Ryanair, easyJet, Vueling connect smaller cities cheaply but check baggage fees.
- **Driving**: International Driving Permit not required for EU within Schengen. Vignette stickers needed in Austria, Switzerland, Czech Republic.
